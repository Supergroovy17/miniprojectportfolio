About me

My name is Brian Suverkrubbe I grew up in Omaha, Nebraska . I love exotic animals, I own six ball pythons 2 bearded dragons and 4 cats. In 2025 I plan on being an exotics breeder and will travel around for reptile shows.My uncle introduced me too exotics as a young boy and ever since Ive had a passion for caring for reptiles . It is interesting to me seeing the differant behaviors of each animal I own ,one of my snakes if he isnt hungry will shake his head no if he doesnt want to eat. I also have a very attention seeking bearded dragon that will make a bunch of noise until I let her out of her cage too play .

I also enjoy anime and cosplay I go too 8 anime conventions a year.I love meeting new people and engaging in con activities with them . My favorite con is Collosal North in The Wisconsin, Dells .
